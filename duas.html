Daily Duas

Authentic supplications from Quran and Sunnah

Morning & Evening Duas

اللَّهُمَّ بِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا وَبِكَ نَحْيَا وَبِكَ نَمُوتُ وَإِلَيْكَ النُّشُورُ

"O Allah, by You we enter the morning and by You we enter the evening, by You we live and by You we die, and to You is the resurrection."

Source: Abu Dawud, Tirmidhi

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لا إِلَهَ إِلا أَنْتَ خَلَقْتَنِي وَأَنَا عَبْدُكَ

"O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no god but You. You created me and I am Your servant."

Source: Sahih Bukhari

Before & After Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ

"In the name of Allah" (Before eating)

Source: Sahih Bukhari

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنِي هَذَا وَرَزَقَنِيهِ مِنْ غَيْرِ حَوْلٍ مِنِّي وَلا قُوَّةٍ

"All praise is due to Allah who fed me this and provided it to me without any strength or power on my part."

Source: Abu Dawud, Tirmidhi

Sleeping & Waking Up

بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

"In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live." (Before sleeping)

Source: Sahih Bukhari

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ

"All praise is due to Allah who gave us life after death, and to Him is the resurrection." (After waking)

Source: Sahih Bukhari

Entering & Leaving Mosque

اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ

"O Allah, open for me the doors of Your mercy." (When entering mosque)

Source: Muslim

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ

"O Allah, I ask You of Your bounty." (When leaving mosque)

Source: Muslim
